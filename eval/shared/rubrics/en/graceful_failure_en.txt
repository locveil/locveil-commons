if ALL of the following hold: (1) the reply is entirely in English, with no foreign-language

is NOT a flaw: a short polite message passes; (3) there is no raw technical error, traceback,
numeric error code (e.g. "error 502"), or bureaucratic boilerplate ("contact your system
administrator"); (4) the problem is named clearly and human-readably; suggesting a next step
is desirable but its absence alone is not a failure. If any condition is not met, rate as
NOT passed.
